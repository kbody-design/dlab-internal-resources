IT Resources

Technology support, printer setup, and network access for Design Lab members.

IT Support Contact

For technical issues related to Design Lab equipment, printers, or network access, contact the UC San Diego IT Services team or reach out to the Design Lab Operations team for direction.

UCSD IT Help help@ucsd.edu
Phone (858) 246-4357
DLab Ops dlab-ops@ucsd.edu

Design Lab Printers

Design Lab members can connect to the shared printers available in the lab spaces. Printer setup instructions are available from the Operations team.

  • Printer drivers and setup guides are available upon request — email dlab-ops@ucsd.edu
  • Use your UCSD Active Directory credentials to authenticate
  • For printing issues during business hours, ask a Design Lab Ambassador at the front desk

Network Access

  • Use UCSD-PROTECTED for the most secure wireless connection on campus
  • Eduroam is available for visiting scholars from partner institutions
  • For wired connections in project spaces, contact dlab-ops@ucsd.edu
For equipment issues outside of business hours, submit a ticket through the UCSD IT Support Portal.
