Operations

Contact the Design Lab Operations team for questions about spaces, policies, and facilities.

Design Lab Operations Team

The Operations team manages day-to-day facilities, space reservations, access requests, and general administrative support for the Design Lab community.

Email dlab-ops@ucsd.edu
Location Design & Innovation Building, 9500 Gilman Drive, MC0425, La Jolla, CA 92093
Hours Monday – Friday, 9:00 am – 5:00 pm

What Operations Can Help With

  • Space access requests and campus card provisioning
  • Conference room and event space reservations
  • Questions about the Design Lab Space Standards and Posting Policy
  • Facilities issues in Design Lab spaces
  • DIB building coordination and reporting
  • Lockout assistance during business hours
  • General questions about Design Lab membership and affiliation
For IT-related issues (printers, network, equipment), please visit IT Resources. For DIB-specific facilities issues, please contact dib-help-g@ucsd.edu and CC dlab-ops@ucsd.edu.
