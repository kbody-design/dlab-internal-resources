Access Information

Campus card access to the Design Lab and DIB by affiliation type.

Design Lab Access Request

Affiliated Design Lab faculty, researchers, and students may request access to the Design Lab. Access is granted through the individual's UC San Diego Campus Card.

Access Provisions by Affiliation

What Access Do I Have?

Access to the Design Lab is dependent on affiliation. Review your category below to understand your provisioned spaces.

Faculty

  • DIB main entrance and elevators — 24/7
  • Assigned Design Lab Project Space (additional spaces require Director's Office approval)
  • DIB Conference Rooms 306 & 307
  • Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 & 361
  • Design Lab Graduate Student Workspace, Room 372

Graduate Students & Post-Docs

  • DIB main entrance and elevators — 24/7
  • Specific Design Lab Project Space (pending approval by Faculty Advisor and/or Project Space Lead)
  • DIB Conference Rooms 306 & 307
  • Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 & 361
  • Design Lab Graduate Student Workspace, Room 372

Research Staff

  • DIB main entrance and elevators — 24/7
  • Specific Design Lab Project Space (pending approval by Faculty Advisor and/or Project Space Lead)
  • DIB Conference Rooms 306 & 307
  • Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 & 361
  • Design Lab Graduate Student Workspace, Room 372

Undergraduate Students

  • DIB main entrance and elevator — daily, 8:00 am – 6:00 pm
  • Project Space access is only provided for students engaged long-term (1 year or more) in a project, specifically requested by an Advisor and approved by Project Space Leads
To request undergraduate project space access, please email Nadir Weibel and Nivardo Valenzuela.

Visiting Scholars

  • DIB main entrance and elevators — 24/7
  • Specific Design Lab Project Space (pending approval by Faculty Advisor and/or Project Space Lead)
  • DIB Conference Rooms 306 & 307
  • Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 & 361
  • Design Lab Graduate Student Workspace, Room 372
Questions about access? Contact the Operations team at dlab-ops@ucsd.edu.
