Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from Design Lab members.

The Design & Innovation Building (DIB) is accessible on weekends, but campus keycard access is required to enter. The building is not staffed on weekends. The Design Lab itself (offices and staffed spaces) is closed on Saturday and Sunday, as well as all UC San Diego University Holidays.

Access is granted through your UC San Diego Campus Card. Submit the Design Lab Access Request Form to initiate the process. Access provisions vary by affiliation — review the Access Information page to understand what spaces you are eligible for. For questions, contact dlab-ops@ucsd.edu.

Affiliated faculty have access to:

  • DIB main entrance and elevators — 24/7
  • Assigned Design Lab Project Space (additional spaces require Director's Office approval)
  • DIB Conference Rooms 306 & 307
  • Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 & 361
  • Design Lab Graduate Student Workspace, Room 372

See the full Access Information page for all affiliation types.

No — Design Lab Conference Rooms 311 and 361 require a reservation. Requests must be submitted a minimum of 2 business days in advance. Confirmation is provided within 1 business day. You can check room availability and submit a request on the Room Reservations page.

Design Lab members are encouraged to subscribe to the Lab's shared calendars to stay informed on recurring events and programming, including:

  • Design @ Large — public lecture series
  • Research Meeting — internal research presentations
  • Open Gov — open governance sessions

Calendar subscription links are available on the Home page.

Tours of the Design Lab and Design & Innovation Building are available for prospective members, collaborators, and visitors. Tours typically include an overview of the project spaces, conference rooms, collaboration areas, and key building amenities. To request a tour, contact dlab-ops@ucsd.edu.

Printer setup instructions and driver downloads are available from the Operations team. Email dlab-ops@ucsd.edu to request setup assistance. You will need your UCSD Active Directory credentials to authenticate. For in-person help during business hours, ask a Design Lab Ambassador at the front desk.

Design Lab Ambassadors staff the front desk during business hours (Monday – Friday, 9:00 am – 5:00 pm) and are the best first point of contact for in-person questions. You can also reach the team by email at dlab-ambassadors@ucsd.edu.

Don't see your question here? Contact the Operations team at dlab-ops@ucsd.edu.
