Reporting Security Incidents

The DIB serves as a central hub for the UC San Diego and greater San Diego community. The safety and security of all building occupants is a top priority.

  • For emergencies, dial (858) 534-4357 to contact campus police
  • To report non-emergency security incidents, use the DIB Incident Form
